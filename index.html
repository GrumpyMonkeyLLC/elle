About

Behind the lens, there's always a story worth telling.

ElleOhElle Media is a one-woman creative force specializing in short film, cinematography, and photography. Based in Ohio, the work pulls from real places, real people, and real light — the kind that only happens for a few minutes at dusk or in the middle of a storm.

Whether it's a personal short film, a brand campaign, or portraits that actually look like the person — the goal is always the same: make something that feels true.
